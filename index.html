How to Play
1. Each round has 2 players.
2. Bet 1 BONE to join (1 bet per round).
3. When 2 bets are in, a random winner is picked (0-4 for #0, 5-9 for #1).
4. Winner gets 1.5 BONE, loser gets an NFT (Mass 1).
5. 0.5 BONE goes to the game owner.
6. New round starts automatically!
7. Merge NFTs to increase Mass (max 10000).
